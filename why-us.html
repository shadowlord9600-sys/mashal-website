Why us

One lab period. Two clear wins.

What students walk away with, and what it asks of the school — which is deliberately almost nothing.

For students

They stop asking AI — and start directing it.

Every session ends with something a student made, not something they watched.

  • Something real to show

    A working website, an automation, a mini-CRM — built by them, in the session. The start of a portfolio years before graduation.

  • Skills past the chatbox

    Prompting is the front door, not the house. Students learn what these tools actually do — and how to make them do it.

  • A sense of direction

    Development, design, data, automation — seeing the paths early enough to steer their subjects and their next steps toward one.

For schools

Everything to gain. Nothing on your plate.

Designed so saying yes costs a school head as little as possible.

  • Your lab, earning its keep

    The hardware is already paid for. An idle period becomes the most talked-about hour on the timetable.

  • Zero burden on staff

    We bring the instructor, curriculum and materials. Your teachers prepare nothing and lose no teaching time.

  • No fee, no procurement

    There is no budget line. And parents see a school teaching the skills everyone else is only talking about.
